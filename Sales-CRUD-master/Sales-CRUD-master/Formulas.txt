Formula for MS Tubes

Oc = outside diameter(mm), Wt = wall thickness(mm)
For square steel tubes: Oc = 4*a
For rectangular steel tubes: Oc = 2a + 2b

Od=Mother Size = (Sum All Sides * 4.4)/14

Kg/m = (Od - Wt) * Wt * 0.02465

Total Weight = (Od - Wt) * Wt * 0.02465 * Length(m)

MS Coil:
7.85g/cm^3 x T(mm) x Width(m) x L(m) = kg
